Comparelection

About

Comparelection is a fairly minimalist site that enables side-by-side comparison of county maps from US Presidential primary and general elections. It began as a simple project in February 2016 to learn D3.js and has hopefully morphed into a useful tool for the politically curious in an election year.

What needs improved?

More Data: The best thing this project could use is simply data from other elections. More general elections will be added in due time since that's just a matter of gathering data that's pretty easy to find. However, finding county-level results for primary elections has been more troublesome. Any help pointing me in the right direction for that data would be much appreciated.

Existing Data: I've recently noticed that some of the 2012 data used from The Guardian seems to be from before all precincts were accounted for, specifically in some of the more populous states. This will be fixed, but the current maps do still paint an almost entirely accurate portrait of the results.

Map Files: All of the GeoJSON paths used on the site are from CivicDashboards, and most of them are excellent. However, some of the states with inland aquatic borders (sorry, Michigan!) look a little goofy. Better paths for those states would also be appreciated.
